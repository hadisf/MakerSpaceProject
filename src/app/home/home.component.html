Our mission

We are an innovation center founded in 2017 in the heart of Prizren, Kosovo. Our mission is to foster innovation, creativity,
and collaboration by providing a unique space where individuals and organizations can come together to share ideas, learn new skills, and develop innovative solutions to today's challenges.

At Makerspace Innovation Center Prizren, we believe that innovation is the key to success in today's rapidly |changing world.
