About us

We help ambitious companies scale to their potential

We’re driven by the belief that technology can be a force for good. Our startups further social impact through programs that reach millions of people around the world.

Our Story

We are an innovation center founded in 2017 in the heart of Prizren, Kosovo. Our mission is to foster innovation, creativity, and collaboration by providing a unique space where individuals and organizations can come together to share ideas, learn new skills, and develop innovative solutions to today's challenges.

At Makerspace Innovation Center Prizren, we believe that innovation is the key to success in today's rapidly changing world. By creating a supportive environment where individuals from different backgrounds and disciplines can collaborate and exchange ideas, we aim to promote the development of new technologies and ideas that will shape the future.

Our center provides a range of resources and services to support innovators, entrepreneurs, and makers in their endeavors. Our facilities include state-of-the-art equipment and tools, co-working spaces, and classrooms for workshops and training sessions. We offer a variety of programs and events designed to help members develop their skills and turn their ideas into reality.
